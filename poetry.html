Wonder Filled Tacos

Tacos

Oh, the wonder filled
tacos!
Oh, the wonder filled
tacos!
Tacos, tacos; wonder filled
tacos!

How I love
to eat tacos,
tacos, tacos!

How I love
to eat tacos
in the evening, and
in the morning!

How I love
to eat tacos,
tacos, tacos!

Delicios delicios
tacos!

Delicios in the
morning
Delicios in the
evening!

Oh, the wonder filled
tacos!
Oh, the wonder filled
tacos!
Tacos, tacos; wonder filled
tacos!
